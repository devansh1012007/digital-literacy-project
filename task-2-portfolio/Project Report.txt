Foundational Digital Portfolio (Requirement: 150-200 words) To build my foundational digital portfolio, I set up profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. I selected GitHub because it is the industry standard for version control and showcasing coding projects to potential employers. LinkedIn was chosen to build my professional network, track my academic milestones, and connect with university alumni and industry professionals. Finally, Kaggle serves as a verifiable record of my problem-solving skills and algorithmic thinking.

Over the next four years of my B.Tech degree, I plan to use these platforms actively rather than passively. I will use GitHub to document my coursework, host my personal side projects, and eventually contribute to open-source repositories. I intend to use LinkedIn to follow tech companies I admire, share my certifications, and search for summer internships. Kaggle will be my primary tool for consistent coding practice, helping me prepare for technical interviews. Together, these three platforms create a comprehensive, professional narrative of my skills and growth.
